Recommendations

The conference venues are located approximately equidistant from two bustling Sheffield neighbourhoods, both of which feature some excellent independent shops, restaurants, pubs and cafes (and the usual chains, if you really must!). Broomhill is located uphill from the conference venues; Hunter’s Bar/Ecclesall ("Eccy") Road is downhill.

Cafes and Coffee Shops

Broomhill

Cawa Coffee - For fresh baked goods, bread, artisan coffees and cool and quirky cafe vibes.

Nam Song﻿ -﻿ Vietnamese street food cafe. Full of cute lanterns and floor seating and offering authentic Vietnamese cuisine and filter coffees, as well as the usual espresso-based options.﻿

Bon -﻿ a beautiful, calming interior, some of the best expresso-based coffees you'll ever drink and some great cakes, pastries and baked goods. The oat milk flat white is particularly good.
